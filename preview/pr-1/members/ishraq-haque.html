Ishraq Haque Research Technician

Ishraq Haque is a research technician in the Chiang Lab. He grew up in South Carolina and graduated from Harvard College in May 2025, earning his bachelor’s degree in Chemistry with a minor in Neuroscience. He also concurrently completed a master’s degree in Chemistry. As a student, he conducted research on the biochemistry of protein post-translational modifications in the context of cancer and epigenetics. He looks forward to investigating the genetic and molecular basis of cardiac arrhythmias in the Chiang Lab. Outside of research, Ishraq enjoys exploring Boston, watching movies, running, and spending time with friends.
